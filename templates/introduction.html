Why simulation modeling?

In the real world, various modeling approaches exist, including mathematical models, physical models, mental models, and spreadsheet-based models. However, specific complex systems require simulation modeling for accurate analysis. Examples include optimizing factory layouts, identifying system bottlenecks, and improving overall system performance.

Traditional approaches like spreadsheets or linear programming solutions often struggle with complexity, especially when systems involve numerous variables, time-dependent dynamics, or non-linear interactions. SimLab aims to provide clients with accurate simulation models or digital twins, closely replicating their real-world manufacturing or logistics systems. By accurately simulating your operations, you gain insights into existing and potential challenges, enabling informed decisions and targeted optimization strategies.
